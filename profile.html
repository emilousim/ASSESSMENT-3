WHO IS EMILY SIMPSON?

AN EXCELLENT ADDITION TO ANY TEAM

  • Aptitude for learning quickly

If it sounds good, regardless of genre, I love it. I also like to attend live concerts and shows as frequently as possible, which has been very little as of late due to COVID-19. In addition to music, I love being with my family (my partner, 4 cats, and 3 dogs), as well as putting together puzzles, and travelling.
